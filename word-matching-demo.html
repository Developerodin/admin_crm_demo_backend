How It Works

The enhanced word matching system uses multiple strategies:

  • Exact Word Match: 3 points for identical words
  • Partial Word Match: 2 points for contained words
  • Word Similarity: 1.5 points for similar words (prefixes, suffixes, character overlap)
  • Type Bonus: +2 points for matching category keywords
  • Action Bonus: +1 point for matching action keywords

Questions need a minimum score of 2 or at least 2 matched words to be considered a match.
